About the Project

"Dogs of Enfield" began as a simple weekend hobby but evolved into a comprehensive documentary study of the relationship between North London's residents and their canine companions.

Inspired by the observational photography tradition of Martin Parr and other British social documentarians, this project captures the everyday moments that reveal the character of a community through its dogs.

From the morning dog walks in Trent Country Park to the afternoon gatherings at Enfield Town Park, these photographs celebrate the joy, companionship, and unique personalities that make up Enfield's vibrant canine culture.
